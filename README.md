# Mechanical Design Projects

A collection of 2D manufacturing and assembly drawings created during academic coursework at AGH. The primary focus of these projects was to apply technical documentation standards, geometric dimensioning and tolerancing (GD&T), and proper assembly visualization.

## Projects

### Drilling Fixture and parts (Academic Project)
- Full mechanical assembly design
- Technical drawings with tolerances and fits

### Winch (Academic Project)
- Shaft, bearings and housing design

### Storage Hook (Academic Project)
- Designed simple mechanical component for industrial use

### Rope Pulley (Academic Project)
- Designed rotating component
